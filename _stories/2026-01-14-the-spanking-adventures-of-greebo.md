## Greebo Time

"It's Greebo time," said Greebo, walking down the sidewalk.  He sauntered into a cafe.

"Wow!  Nice barista!" Greebo said towards the barista.  The barista blushed.  Greebo walked towards her, shuffling his feet.

"I'll have a Greebo latte, hold the sugar, no ice, just coffee please!"

"Amazing order," said the barista, blushing once again.  She utilized her fancy Italian espresso machine to make the elite coffee order.

"Here you go!" she said, smiling, handing the latte to Greebo.  Greebo frowned.  The barista had added a single ice cube.  Greebo placed the latte back on the counter.

"What did I say would happen if you put ice in my coffee again?" Greebo asked the barista.  The barista gulped nervously.  She had added the ice cube on purpose, because she knew the consequences, but now she was still nervous.

"I... uh... would get my bottom spanked."

"That's right," said Greebo.  With a single, delicate leap, he cleared the counter, lifted up the barista's apron and skirt, and flipped her indelicately over her stool.

"It's Greebo time!"

## The Spanking

Greebo rained down slaps upon the upturned bottom of the barista.  Dang, her bottom was getting hot!  The barista twisted and turned, but she couldn't escape the just and necessary spanking that her bottom was being subjected to.

"Ouchie!" the barista yelped.

"You'll think twice before popping a cube in a Greebo latte in the future," lectured Greebo.

"Oh... I'll think twice for sure," the barista smugly replied.

"Are you enjoying this?" asked Greebo.
