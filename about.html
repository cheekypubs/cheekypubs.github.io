About Cheeky.pub

What We Are

Cheeky.pub is a curated library of spanking fiction written by and for adults who share an interest in consensual discipline stories. We're a community-run project that aims to give writers a home for their work and readers a place to discover new stories.

For Readers

Browse our growing collection of stories by author, tag, or just scroll through the latest additions. Whether you're into historical settings, contemporary drama, or something more lighthearted, we aim to host a diverse range of voices and scenarios. All stories are clearly tagged so you can find what appeals to you.

For Writers

We welcome submissions from anyone in the community. Stories can be standalone or multi-chapter series, and we support Markdown formatting for easy writing. If you'd like to contribute, head over to our submission page for details.
